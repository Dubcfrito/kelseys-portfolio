About Me

I’m Kelsey Cooper, a 2D Animator and Concept Artist based in Tempe, Arizona. In my freetime, I enjoy cuddling with my kitty, Bentley, playing video games, and watching movies.

I bring the passion to animation, and my drawing tablet brings the pixels! If you have a vision in mind, I’m your guy to help bring it to life.

I have experience working as a 2D Character Animator for several projects, including the Toon Sludge Podcast and Daughter of the Sea. I am also currently developing my own short film, The Necrosis Protocol, for my thesis at Arizona State University where I currently attend.

Animation isn’t just my work — it’s the heartbeat that drives me!
